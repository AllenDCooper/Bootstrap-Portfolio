About Me

I am an editor who works in educational publishing at Macmillan Learning here in NYC. I live in West Chelsea with my wife Meredith, who I met when we were both undergraduates at the University of Chicago. (At the time, we were both secretly aspiring writers–and still are!) I've lived in NYC for nearly two years, after living in South Florida for nearly three years. I'm originally from Newburyport, MA.

I've worked in publishing, in some form or another, for over fifteen years, going back to when I started a school newspaper with two of my classmates back in high school. I consider myself a bibliophile and a technophile! Call it media pluralism. I believe our media diet should be as intentional as our food diet. Speaking of diet, I am a vegan, who loves coffee in the morning and drinking wine in the evening–especially around a campfire, in Central Park, or at my favorite wine spot Terroir in Tribeca.
